Membership Selection

Associate Realtor (AR) — Entry Level

Recommended for anyone with 0–1 year of real estate experience. Annual Fee: KSh 7,500. Application Fee: KSh 2,500 (non-refundable).

Professional Realtor (PR)

For realtors with 1+ years experience. Three levels: Junior (JR), Senior (SR), and Certified Professional Realtor (CPR). Annual Fee: KSh 12,500. Application Fee: KSh 2,500 (non-refundable).

Broker

Business owners who have employed a certified AR or PR. Annual Fee: KSh 20,000. Application Fee: KSh 2,500 (non-refundable).

Corporate

Open to banks, insurance, developers and service providers. Annual Fee: KSh 50,000. Application Fee: KSh 2,500 (non-refundable).

Affiliate

Our sponsors and partners — chapters, subsidiaries, MOUs and linkages. Annual Fee: KSh 50,000. Application Fee: KSh 2,500 (non-refundable).
