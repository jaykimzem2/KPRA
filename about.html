Who We Are

Kenya Professional Realtors Association

Kenya Professional Realtors Association (KPRA), incorporated in 2013, is a professional organization for Real Estate professionals who sell, buy and manage Real Estate property and businesses.

KPRA provides a variety of services to its members including education, training, certification, affinity programs and legislative representation, as well as membership in local and regional associations affiliated with KPRA.

To work on behalf of Kenyan property owners, K.P.R.A provides a facility for professional development, research and exchange of information among its members and to the public and government for the purpose of preserving the free enterprise system and transfer real property.

Mission, Vision & Core Values

Our Mission

To be the collective force influencing and shaping the real estate industry. To be the leading advocate of the right to own, use and transfer real property. To be the acknowledged leader in developing standards for efficient, effective and ethical real estate business practices. To create training and certification for members, offer networking opportunities and cooperate with government agencies in improving the Real Estate industry.

Our Vision

The core purpose of K.P.R.A is advancing real estate professional services by way of promoting its members through training, certification and professional designation. We envision a Kenya where every real estate transaction is conducted with the highest standards of professionalism, transparency and integrity.

Core Values

Integrity — Uncompromising ethics Professionalism — World-class standards Excellence — Always improving Innovation — Progressive thinking Collaboration — Industry partnerships Advocacy — Members first

What We Do

Our Key Services

Education & Training

Continuous professional development programs, workshops and the Certified Professional Realtor (CPR) certification course — creating a platform for success.

Certification Programs

The KPRA CPR designation is Kenya's premier real estate certification, distinguishing our members as trusted, qualified professionals in the market.

Membership Network

Monthly power networking events connecting members with clients, developers, financiers and allied professionals across Kenya's real estate ecosystem.

Legislative Advocacy

Active representation in policy dialogue and legislative processes affecting real estate — ensuring a fair, transparent and regulated industry for all.

Publications

The Professional Realtor Magazine, Real Estate Reports and Newsletters deliver timely market intelligence and professional insights to our members.

Awards & Recognition

Kenya's largest annual real estate awards and recognition event — celebrating excellence and setting benchmarks for the entire industry.
