What is MLS?

A Unified Marketplace

The KPRA Market Listing Service (MLS) is a professional platform where members and non-members can list, buy, and sell properties across Kenya. It serves as a central hub that connects sellers with potential buyers through a network of accredited real estate professionals.

By using the KPRA MLS, you tap into a verified database of property listings, ensuring transparency, professional standards, and faster transaction times for both realtors and property seekers.
