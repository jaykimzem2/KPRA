What You Get

Our Benefits Vary by Membership Category

KPRA membership unlocks a comprehensive suite of benefits designed to grow your career and business.

Associate & Professional Members

Individual membership is open to all practicing Real Estate professionals.

  • Opportunity to participate in K.P.R.A monthly power networking events
  • Market knowledge information and industry intelligence
  • Legislative and community initiatives representation
  • Participating in annual events, awards celebration and recognition
  • Invitations to annual conventions and celebrations
  • Inclusion in the Professional Network Directory
  • Free access to KPRA Real Estate Directory
  • Free mailing of the Professional Realtors Magazine
  • Opportunity to advertise in the Magazine at discounted rates
  • Continuous professional development programmes
  • Discounts on K.P.R.A conferences and seminars
  • CPR Certification Eligibility (Professional Members)

Brokers, Corporate & Affiliate Members

Corporate membership is open to banks, insurance companies, real estate developers and allied service providers.

  • Opportunity to participate in K.P.R.A monthly power networking events
  • Market knowledge information and industry intelligence
  • Legislative and community initiatives representation
  • Participating in annual events, awards celebration and recognition
  • Invitations to annual conventions and celebrations
  • Inclusion in the Professional Network Directory
  • Free access to KPRA Real Estate Directory
  • Free mailing of the Professional Realtors Magazine
  • Opportunity to advertise in the Magazine at discounted rates
  • Continuous professional development programmes
  • Discounts on K.P.R.A conferences and seminars
  • Company brand visibility and co-marketing opportunities
  • Expo and exhibition participation rights
  • KPRA Association endorsement and recognition
