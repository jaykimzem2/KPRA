Overview

What is Power Networking?

Power Networking is a signature KPRA initiative that brings together members and non-members to connect, collaborate, and grow their real estate networks. We believe that in the property industry, your network is your net worth.

Each month, we host exclusive sessions where realtors, developers, financiers, and service providers meet to share market insights, explore partnership opportunities, and facilitate high-value property transactions in a professional yet relaxed atmosphere.

Join KPRA for Full Access
Exclusive Opportunity

Become a KPRA Patron — Your Gateway to Exclusive Real Estate Networking

As a KPRA Patron, you gain access to some of Kenya's most powerful real estate networking events without being tied down to a full membership.

You'll be in the room with certified realtors, investors, developers, and industry leaders. Patrons enjoy access to Power Networking events, award galas, open houses, corporate golf events, and more. This is an ideal entry point for professionals, entrepreneurs, and investors who want to build meaningful connections in Kenya's real estate space.
